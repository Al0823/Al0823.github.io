This page is the character info page for Amda from the Murder Drones series.
Overview of Amda

Amda was a minor character in Murder Drones and was voiced by Lizzie Freeman. Amda was one of the Worker Drone test subjects at Cabin Fever Labs who was killed by Alice after she was bootlooped and attacked by one of the Sentinels.

Amda's Physical Appearance

Amda has neon green eyes and dark green short hair. She wears white gloves, a black hardhat, a short black dress and a black collar on her neck bearing her ID number, similar to Yeva, Nori and Alice. Furthermore, she is barefoot.

Amda's Personality

Not much is known about Amda's personality, but she was seen scared the whole time she's made her appearance because she was in the Cabin Fever Labs, surrounded by the Sentinels.

Amda's Biography

Dead End

She first appeared when the computer zooms out in the beginning of the episode. The camera goes down and the viewer sees her hiding from the Sentinels behind Dean's [023] cubicle wall, she crawled forward trying to save another Worker Drone, and almost succeeded, when unfortunately the Sentinels caught up and brutally killed the said Worker Drone. A few seconds later, a sentinel found her by looking above the cubicle wall behind Amda. She yelled and fell to the floor trying to crawl away, but was bootlooped and paralyzed. The sentinel went over the wall and attacked Amda, eating her in the process.

She reappears later in the episode, getting decapitated by Alice. Her head falls into a bucket filled with oil with a piece of tape with the word "heads" on it. As her head falls into the bucket, oil spills out of the bucket.

Mass Destruction

Amda doesn't re-appear in Mass Destruction, but a holding cell with a door that has her numerical designation and name is briefly visible during the episode.
