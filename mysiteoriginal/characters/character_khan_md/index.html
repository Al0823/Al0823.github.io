Overview of Khan Doorman

Khan is a supporting character in the Murder Drones series, he is voiced by David J. Dixon in the English version of the series. He is the father of Uzi Doorman, the previous estranged husband of Nori Doorman and the inventor of the blast doors that kept the colony safe from Disassembly Drones.
