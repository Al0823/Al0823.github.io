Role: Supporting Character
Family: Father of Uzi Doorman
Inventor: Blast doors protecting the colony from Disassembly Drones
Voiced by: David J. Dixon (English)
Overview of Khan Doorman

is a supporting character in the Murder Drones series. He is the father of Uzi Doorman, the previous estranged husband of Nori Doorman, and the inventor of the blast doors that kept the colony safe from Disassembly Drones.
